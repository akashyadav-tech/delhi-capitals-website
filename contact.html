Frequently Asked Questions

How can I buy tickets for Delhi Capitals matches?

Tickets for Delhi Capitals home matches can be purchased through our official ticketing partner BookMyShow, at the stadium box office, or through authorized ticketing agents. Online booking typically opens 2-3 weeks before each match.

Where can I buy official Delhi Capitals merchandise?

Official Delhi Capitals merchandise is available at our stadium store during match days, through our online store on the official website, and at select retail partners across India. You can also purchase merchandise through our official Amazon store.

How can I apply for membership in the official fan club?

Membership applications for the official Delhi Capitals fan club are typically open during the pre-season period. You can apply through our official website. Members get exclusive benefits including early ticket access, member-only merchandise, and special fan events.

Are stadium tours available at Arun Jaitley Stadium?

Yes, guided stadium tours are available on non-match days. Tours include visits to the dressing rooms, practice facilities, and the museum. Booking can be done through our website or by contacting the stadium administration office.

How can I get player autographs or meet the team?

We organize fan meet-and-greet events throughout the season. Keep an eye on our social media channels and website for announcements about such events. Player autograph sessions are also organized after certain matches at designated areas.

How to Reach Arun Jaitley Stadium

By Metro: Take the Violet Line to Delhi Gate Metro Station (Gate No. 3) or the Blue Line to JLN Stadium Metro Station.

By Bus: DTC buses stop at Delhi Gate, Kashmere Gate, and ITO. The stadium is within walking distance from these stops.

By Car/Taxi: The stadium is easily accessible from all parts of Delhi. Parking is available at designated areas around the stadium.

Parking: Limited parking is available at the stadium. We recommend using public transport on match days.
